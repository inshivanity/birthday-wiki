Thea Maneckshaw (born 11 June 2000) is a teacher, musician, artist, Formula One enthusiast and emotional phenomenon. She is best known for her work in education, participation in the musical groups Clefhangers and Take Fifty-Six, and her ability to experience approximately 86 emotions simultaneously.

Early life [edit]

Thea Maneckshaw was born on 11 June 2000, reportedly earlier than expected after hearing the sound of go-karts and deciding she wished to participate. A small, curly-haired infant, she had already established a close personal relationship with poop before birth.

She was famously born with shitty lungs, which she credits for her long-standing relationship with potty.

She is known to have fallen asleep to Bryan Adams as a baby. In 2004 she was introduced to Pixar's Cars, an event widely regarded as one of the most significant moments of her development.

Education [edit]

Maneckshaw attended Nowrosjee Wadia College. Accounts of her attendance vary considerably, though surviving records suggest she was at least aware that classes were taking place.[12]

Music [edit]

She was a member of Clefhangers between 2018 and 2019 and remains associated with Take Fifty-Six. In addition to singing, she plays guitar, writes songs, paints, crafts and generally proves that "I can do that myself" is less a statement and more a lifestyle.

College years [edit]

During her third day of college, Maneckshaw met Shivani Puranik ("Kronk"), an event now considered historically significant. The pair quickly formed a friendship characterized by relentless yapping, collaborative singing, mutual support, and making each other laugh until tears occurred (primarily on Thea's side).

Other academic activities are believed to have occurred during this period. Mathematics may have been involved.

Career and adult life [edit]

Maneckshaw currently works as a teacher and has been described by multiple sources as "The Best Teacher to Exist." She successfully navigated the challenges of adulthood, including Getting a Job, Doing the Job, and Not Making Enough Money Despite Having a Job.

Interests [edit]

Her interests include Formula One, dinosaurs, astronomy, and the television series Dark.

Favourite foods include kheema par eedu, akuri, bibimbap, burritos and babka. Researchers have concluded that she is, in fact, a true eedu lover.

Favourite drinks include apple cider, cherry coke, coconut water and banana caramel shakes.

Favourite bands include Arctic Monkeys, Peter Cat Recording Co., Def Leppard and Waterparks.

Driving [edit]

Maneckshaw is widely regarded as an exceptional driver. Alternative career paths frequently proposed include Formula One driver and ride-share operator ("Oovoo Javer").

Legacy [edit]

Friends and family consistently describe Maneckshaw as fiercely loyal, deeply compassionate and incapable of loving halfway. She is known not only for sharing in the excitement of others, but for amplifying it.

There are very few people in the world who are genuinely and undoubtedly good. Contemporary scholarship places Maneckshaw firmly within that category.

As of 2025, researchers have been unable to determine whether Thea's capacity for love, enthusiasm, emotional support and tears has an upper limit.
